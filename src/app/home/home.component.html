What is Car Insurance?


Car insurance, also referred to as motor or auto insurance, is an insurance policy that provides you financial protection if your vehicle suffers damages due to accidents, natural disasters, or thefts.

With a Comprehensive Car Insurance Policy, you will be financially secured in case of any losses to your vehicle due to an unfortunate event. Apart from that, a private car insurance policy also covers third-party liabilities and ensures that you comply with the law of the land.

Whether you are looking to buy a policy to comply with the legal norms or looking for comprehensive coverage, We have it all! We offer insurance policies, including third-party car insurance, comprehensive car insurance, and own-damage car insurance online at incredibly low premiums.


What's Covered Under Car Insurance Policy

Here are the inclusions of the ACKO Comprehensive Car Insurance Policy. Please refer to the policy wordings for more information about the policy coverage.


Third-Party Losses

If your car causes damages to third-party property/vehicle or life, such losses are covered by the Car Insurance Policy.

Accidents

Damages caused to your car due to accidents/collisions are covered under car insurance.

Fire

Damages caused to your car due to accidental fire, vandalism, self-ignition, etc., are covered by the Comprehensive Plan.


Theft

Car theft is common in India, and you could face a heavy financial loss if your car gets stolen. Comprehensive Policy includes this coverage.

Calamities

Damages from both natural and man-made calamities are covered under a Comprehensive Car Insurance Policy.

Rat Bite Damages

Damage to your car due to rat bites can lead to significant repairs. It is covered under our four-wheeler insurance policy.




What's Not Covered Under Car Insurance Policy

Here are the exclusions of the ACKO Car Insurance Policy. Please refer to the policy wordings for an exhaustive list of exclusions.


Pre-Existing Damages

Any damage that your car has suffered before buying a policy from us will not be covered.

Normal Wear and Tear

The repair or replacement cost is not covered under the car insurance plan for those car parts that suffer damage over time due to regular use.

Mechanical and Electrical Damage

Any type of manufacturing defect in the car’s component will not be covered under our Comprehensive Car Insurance Policy.


Driving Without Valid Documents

Having the necessary documents while driving is important. Car damage, while not having such documents (active Driving Licence, vehicle RC, etc.), is not covered under the car insurance policy.

Illegal Driving

Driving your car in an illegal race, driving under the influence, or engaging in a crime is called illegal driving. The ACKO car insurance plan does not cover car damages caused during such instances.

Loss Outside Geographical Limits

Any damage to the car when driving outside the geographical limits of India is not covered under car insurance.
